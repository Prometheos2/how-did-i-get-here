This add-on will never send any personal information to the add-on author or any third-party.

To be able to work, it requests the favicons (the icons at your tab) from the websites, which are displayed in the tab history.

An explanation of all permissions, this add-on requests, can be found at https://github.com/rugk/how-did-i-get-here/blob/master/assets/texts/en/permissions.md.
